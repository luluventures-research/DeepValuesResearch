**Moat Quality: WIDE (Narrowing Gap with Nvidia, Widening Gap with Intel)**

*   **A. Intangible Assets (The x86 License):** AMD holds one of only two major licenses to produce x86 CPUs (the other is Intel). This is a virtually insurmountable barrier to entry for new competitors in the PC/Server legacy market.

*   **D. Pricing Power:** Evidence exists. Gross margins have expanded from ~40% (2019) to **54.2% (2025)**, proving they can raise prices while gaining market share.
